About

Curious by nature,
deliberate by practice.

I'm Yagnik — an ICT Engineering student with a deep interest in how technology, storytelling, and human behavior intersect. I build things that are functional, but I care equally about the meaning behind them.

Outside coursework in robotics, communications systems, and probability, I spend time writing opinion pieces, editing videos in DaVinci Resolve, and reading broadly — psychology, philosophy, geopolitics. The thread connecting it all: understanding how people think and how ideas move through the world.

Long-term, I want to build something — a product, a film, a company — that stands at the intersection of technology and genuine human value.
